Research & Impact

Advancing environmental health through interdisciplinary research in WASH, infectious disease control, and health systems strengthening.

Research Experience

CIHR Health Systems Impact Fellow

BC Ministry of Health, Sciences and Guidelines Development (Sep 2024 - Aug 2025)

Focus: Investigating failure risks and health hazards in BC's water distribution systems to inform policy and best practices.

Graduate Research Assistant

Environmental Systems Engineering, University of British Columbia (Sep 2021 - Present)

Supervisor: Prof. Sara E. Beck. Research on virucidal effects of disinfectants/UV/sunlight on biofilms, microbial community analysis, biofilm characterization (CLSM, ATP), and Mpox virus inactivation studies informing WHO policy.

Postdoctoral Researcher

Civil and Environmental Health Dept., Tufts University (Sep 2018 - Jun 2019)

Supervisor: Prof Daniele Lantagne. Led research on CISUR interventions in emergencies, evaluated Goma UFPSE water program (cholera response), analyzed Haiti WASH programs, studied membrane filter fouling, and collaborated on novel ceramic water filter development (Kohler partnership).

Co-Investigator, Baby WASH project

Johns Hopkins University School of Public Health / Food for the Hungry (Aug 2017 - Sep 2018)

PI: Prof Christine M George. Investigated links between environmental enteropathies and child development in DRC. Coordinated implementation, training, data management, and dissemination. Resulted in 12 peer-reviewed articles.

Research Coordinator

Regional School of Public Health (ERSP), DRC / Ghent University (Dec 2014 - Aug 2017)

Spearheaded design and implementation of 10,000-participant NCD cohort studies (Observ & DSS). Partnered with Ghent University-Vlir-UOS - UCB on advanced research projects.

Research Assistant

The Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel (Dec 2014 - Aug 2017)

Advisor: Prof Jeremy D. Kark. Investigated heavy metal exposure in Israelis and Palestinians via toenail clippings and associations with Paraoxonase 1.

Work Experience Highlights

Consultant - Global Health Initiatives

World Health Organization (WHO) Emergencies Program, Geneva, Switzerland (Jun 2019 - Aug 2023)

Advised on IPC/WASH guidelines for outbreak-prone regions during health emergencies (e.g., Ebola, Marburg, Mpox). Provided expertise in outbreak management, developed toolkits, advised Ministries of Health, developed M&E tools, supported policy analysis, and coordinated partners.

Research Scholar & Project Coordinator

Tufts University, Medford (Boston), MA, USA (Sep 2018 - Jun 2019)

Conducted action research/evaluations of WASH interventions (DRC, Haiti), led lab studies on filter fouling/biofilm, collaborated on cholera prevention/emergency response.

Project Coordinator & Researcher

Food for the Hungry / Johns Hopkins University, DRC/USA (Sep 2017 - Sep 2018)

Led staff in a \$2M USAID-funded Baby WASH RCT. Designed community/lab protocols, developed database systems, trained field staff.

Academic Coordinator & Lecturer

Regional School of Public Health (ERSP) - UCB, DRC (Dec 2014 - Sep 2017)

Managed academic programs, led NCD cohort studies, fostered international collaborations, mentored students, conducted workshops.

Physician & Medical Information Department Manager

Provincial Hospital HPGRB, DRC (2012-2013, 2014-2017)

Provided clinical care, designed surveillance systems (antibiotic resistance, MRSA), clinical research, mentored students, developed/managed hospital information system.
